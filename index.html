WHAT IS HACKOVERFLOW?

HackoverFlow is the flagship hackathon conducted by Cyscom VIT Chennai formerly OWASP VIT Chennai. HackoverFlow encourages participants to present their solutions to tackle challenging real-world problems. It is a platform to compete and network with like-minded and skilled students from all walks of life.

HackoverFlow is an 48hr hackathon event, themed on latest technological requirements of NGOs, and startups, focusing on the domains of blockchain, AI & ML, IOT etc. So gather your teams and get ready for the ultimate technical-skills showdown from 23rd September (Friday) to 25th September(Sunday).
